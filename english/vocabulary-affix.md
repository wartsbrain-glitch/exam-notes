# 词根词缀

## 常见前缀

| 前缀 | 含义 | 例词 |
|------|------|------|
| un-/in-/im-/dis- | 否定 | unhappy, impossible, disagree |
| re- | 再次 | review, renew |
| pre- | 在...之前 | preview, predict |
| post- | 在...之后 | postgraduate |
| inter- | 在...之间 | international, interact |
| trans- | 跨越 | transport, transform |
| super- | 超过 | superman, supermarket |

## 常见词根

| 词根 | 含义 | 例词 |
|------|------|------|
| spect | 看 | inspect, respect, spectator |
| dict | 说 | predict, dictionary, contradict |
| port | 携带 | transport, export, portable |
| struct | 建造 | construct, structure, instruct |

---

> 掌握词根词缀，遇到生词也能猜个大概。
