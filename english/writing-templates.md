# 万能句型

## 开头段

### 图画/图表描述

- As is vividly depicted in the picture, ...
- The chart above clearly illustrates that ...

### 引出话题

- When it comes to ..., people's opinions vary from person to person.
- In recent years, ... has become a heated topic.

## 中间段

### 论证观点

- There is no denying that ...
- A convincing example is that ...
- From my perspective, ... can be attributed to the following factors.

### 利弊分析

- Every coin has two sides. On the one hand, ...; on the other hand, ...
- The advantages of ... are obvious. First, ... Second, ...

## 结尾段

### 总结建议

- In conclusion, it is high time that we took effective measures to ...
- Only in this way can we ...
