## 常用公式速查

### 极限

$$\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1$$

$$\lim_{x \to 0} (1 + x)^{\frac{1}{x}} = e$$

### 导数

$$(\sin x)' = \cos x, \quad (\cos x)' = -\sin x$$

$$(\ln x)' = \frac{1}{x}, \quad (e^x)' = e^x$$
